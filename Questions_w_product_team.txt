Part - 02

Gaps to raise with the product team

When inventory moves between two warehouses, should that be one event or two separate transfer_out / transfer_in log rows?

Should negative stock ever be permitted (backorders), or is the CHECK (quantity >= 0) always correct?

When a bundle is sold, does inventory deduct from each component separately, or does the bundle itself hold stock?

Who controls the product catalog — the platform, or each company individually?

Should each warehouse have its own staff, or do company-level roles apply across all warehouses?
